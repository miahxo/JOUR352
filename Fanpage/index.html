Presenting One Of the Best Female Artists: H.E.R.

Gabriella Sarmiento Wilson, known as H.E.R., is an American R&B singer-songwriter.

Check her out on her official website.

Why is she one of my favorites?

  1. I fell in love with her after hearing her song "Comfortable," which was released in 2020.

  2. Her first album, "H.E.R.," helped me get through middle school when I went through tough times. My favorite song from this album is "Losing."

  3. I love how she carries herself! She uses her musical platform to address social issues. An example of this is her George Floyd tribute song, "I Can't Breathe."
